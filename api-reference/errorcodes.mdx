This section contains a list of possible errors you might come across when using our API and how you can resolve the error.



\| \*\*Error code\*\* | \*\*HTTP Status\*\* | \*\*Meaning\*\*                                    | \*\*Solution\*\*                                        |

\| -------------- | --------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |

\| 3              | 401             | Failed Authentication                         | Ensure you entered valid authentication keys.      |

\| 5              | 422             | Invalid parameters.                           | Ensure you entered the correct parameters.         |

\| 20             | 422             | Invalid date range.                           | Ensure the date range is within 14 days.           |

\| 22             | 400             | Invalid page number                           | Ensure the page number is within the range of 1 to 100. |

\| 25             | 449             | You have exceeded the rate limit.             | Try again later.                                   |

\| 34             | 404             | The resource you requested could not be found  | Ensure the requested resource information is correct. |

\| 47             | 400             | Invalid input                                  | Ensure your input value is valid.                  |
